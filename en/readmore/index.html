ReadMore.js
Simple and Effective Pure JS Solution

A pure JavaScript plugin that helps collapse long text blocks. With «Read more» and «Close» buttons, you can easily show or hide additional content, making reading more convenient and saving space on the page. This plugin is ideal for mobile devices and helps to manage large amounts of text comfortably.

npm i @corgras/readmore-js
