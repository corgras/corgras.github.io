ReadMore.js
Simple and Effective Pure JS Solution

A pure JavaScript plugin that helps collapse long text blocks. With «Read More» and «Hide» buttons, you can easily show or hide additional content, making reading convenient and saving space on the page. The plugin is perfect for mobile devices and handles large volumes of text efficiently.

npm i @corgras/readmore-js
